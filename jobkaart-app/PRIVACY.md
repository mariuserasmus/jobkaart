# JobKaart Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 29, 2025
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026

## Introduction

JobKaart ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use JobKaart.

## POPIA Compliance

We are fully compliant with POPIA (Act 4 of 2013) and respect your rights as a data subject under South African law.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign up for JobKaart, we collect:
- Your name
- Business name
- Email address
- Phone number (WhatsApp)
- Business address (optional)
- VAT registration (if applicable)

### 2. Customer Data You Enter
You control what customer information you add to JobKaart:
- Customer names
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Physical addresses
- Custom notes

### 3. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Login times and frequency
- Features used
- Device type and browser
- IP address (for security)

### 4. Payment Information
We use PayFast to process payments. We **never** store your credit card details. PayFast is PCI-DSS compliant.

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Provide the service**: Store your quotes, jobs, invoices, and customer data
- **Send notifications**: Email/WhatsApp alerts about quotes viewed, invoices overdue, etc.
- **Customer support**: Help you troubleshoot issues
- **Billing**: Process your monthly subscription

### We Will NEVER:
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Share your customer list with anyone
- ❌ Use your data for advertising
- ❌ Contact your customers directly
- ❌ Share data with competitors

## Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

1. **Access** - Request a copy of all data we hold about you
2. **Correction** - Request we correct inaccurate data
3. **Deletion** - Request we delete your account and all data
4. **Objection** - Object to how we process your data
5. **Portability** - Export your data in CSV/JSON format
6. **Withdraw consent** - Unsubscribe from marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email: **privacy@jobkaart.co.za**

We will respond within **30 days** as required by POPIA.

## Data Storage & Security

### Where We Store Data
- **Cloud Provider**: Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- **Location**: Data centers in Europe (with backup in US)
- **Backups**: Daily automated backups, retained for 30 days

### Security Measures
- ✅ **Encryption in transit** - All data sent over HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
- ✅ **Encryption at rest** - Database encrypted at rest
- ✅ **Access control** - Only you can access your data
- ✅ **Password security** - Bcrypt hashing for passwords
- ✅ **Two-factor authentication** - Optional 2FA for your account
- ✅ **Regular security audits** - Quarterly penetration testing

### Quote & Invoice Links
- Public links (for WhatsApp sharing) use **tokenized URLs**
- Links **expire after 90 days** of inactivity
- No personal information in the URL itself
- Can be revoked/regenerated at any time

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- You can delete individual customers, quotes, or jobs anytime

### Cancelled Accounts
- After cancellation, your data is:
  - **Soft-deleted**: Invisible to you, retained for 30 days in case you change your mind
  - **Hard-deleted**: Permanently removed after 30 days
  - **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days

### Legal Requirements
- If required by law (e.g., tax audit), we may retain invoices for up to **7 years**
- You will be notified if this applies to you
