## The "Trust Ladder" Strategy

Move people up the trust ladder:

### Level 1: Awareness (Low Trust)
- They see your post in a Facebook group
- They visit the landing page
- **Conversion rate: 1-2%** (expect 1 signup per 100 visitors)

### Level 2: Consideration (Medium Trust)
- Someone they know mentioned it
- They see a testimonial from someone like them
- **Conversion rate: 10-15%**

### Level 3: Decision (High Trust)
- A friend personally recommended it
- You offer to set it up FOR them
- They see you're active\responsive
- **Conversion rate: 40-60%**

**Your goal:** Move people from Level 1 â†’ Level 3 as fast as possible.

## What to Do When People Say "I'll Think About It"

### If they say: "Let me think about it"
**Your response:**
> "No stress. Can I ask - what's making you hesitate? Is it the price, the setup, or something else? I'm genuinely trying to make this as useful as possible."

### If they say: "I don't trust putting my data online"
**Your response:**
> "I get it - I'd feel the same. Here's the thing: your data is encrypted with the same security banks use. And unlike a notebook in your bakkie, if something happens to your phone, your data is safe in the cloud. But I totally understand if you'd rather stick with what you know."

### If they say: "What if you shut down the app?"
**Your response:**
> "Fair question. I'm committed to this for at least 2 years - I've already invested [X hours/money] into building it. And if I ever did shut down, I'd give everyone 60 days notice and a way to export their data. But honestly, I'm just getting started."

### If they say: "I'll sign up later"
**Your response:**
> "Cool - I'll check in with you in 2 weeks. In the meantime, keep track of how many quotes you send that you forget to follow up on. That's the number that JobKaart is designed to fix."

## When You'll Know It's Working

### Good Signs:
- People message YOU asking about JobKaart (not the other way around)
- Users refer friends WITHOUT you asking

**If you see bad signs:** Talk to users. Ask them what's missing or broken. Fix it before trying to get more signups.
