## How to Use for Facebook Ads

1. **Open the HTML file** in your browser
2. **Adjust browser zoom** to fit the ad nicely on screen
3. **Take a screenshot** (Windows: Win + Shift + S)
4. **Upload to Facebook Ads Manager**
5. **Target**:
   - Location: South Africa
   - Interests: Small business, plumbing, electrician, handyman, trades
   - Age: 25-55
   - Language: English, Afrikaans

---

## Ad Performance Tips

### Best Performing Elements:
- ✅ Direct pain points (lost money, forgotten quotes)
- ✅ South African language ("bakkie", "tannie", "R" currency)
- ✅ Clear FREE messaging
- ✅ Simple value prop (5 quotes, jobs, invoices)
- ✅ No credit card required

### A/B Testing Ideas:
- Test "New Year" ads vs "Practical" ads
- Test "Fear of loss" vs "Promise of gain"
- Test different CTA buttons (Start FREE vs Get Started)

## Color Psychology Used

- **Orange/Red**: Urgency, action, lost money
- **Green**: Success, money saved, growth
- **Blue**: Trust, professional, reliable
- **Yellow**: Warning, attention, important
- **Dark backgrounds**: Premium feel, seriousness
